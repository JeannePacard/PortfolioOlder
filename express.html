Audio and video production

As part of an audiovisual course, I directed and edited a video. I chose the music Tomorrow by Jorja Smith because it is a music that particularly touches me. It tells the story of a person who must leave in order to heal despite the love that can be brought to him where he is. That's what I tried to convey through the image.

Poster Portrait of a lady on Fire

Create, compose and edit visuals

After seeing the movie "Portrait of a Girl on Fire" and analyzing the official poster of the movie, I tried to create one that corresponds to the movie in my opinion. On top I represented page 28, it has a great importance since it is on this page that Marianne draws herself and seals her love with Heloise. I also represented Marianne and Heloise themselves in the form of a drawing because it is their love that is the subject of the whole film.

fight against binge drinking

Create, compose and edit visuals
Audio and video production

Before we started our Art and Science project, we did campaigns against binge drinking. We had a time constraint: we had to make 8 Instagram posts in 24 hours. We decided to make like a "vlog" of a party where we see the evolution of the state of a person during a party until the end so that the viewer can put himself in the place of the person and see the dangers.
