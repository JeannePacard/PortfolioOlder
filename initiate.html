At the beginning of our first year, we had to organize my graduation ceremony. With my group, we organized a Halloween themed ceremony. We had to organize the event, come up with activities and manage the food while respecting an imposed budget. We put our project management classes to work by making a GANTT.

reduce menstrual precariousness

Managing a project with a traditional method
Budgeting a project and monitoring its profitability

To reduce menstrual insecurities, three classmates and I sought to make sanitary pads available at our IUT. To do so, we designed "homemade" racks using recycled materials such as bed slats to create the feet! In order to fill the racks with sanitary products, we contacted the nurse at the IUT. She provided us with tampons as well as checks dedicated to feminine hygiene, which allowed us to fill the racks. So we were able to make the pads available to everyone who needed them.
